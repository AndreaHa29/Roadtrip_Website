Washington Roadtrip

This will be a 10 day long roadtrip through Washington. We're stopping by 3 locations Whidbey Island, Port Townsend, and Port Angeles to visit Washington landmarks, try new restaurants, and explore nature. This trip includes hiking, mueseum tours, and art gallerys that each town is known for. The total travel time is about 6 hours round trip but this will be split up across multiple days and ferry rides.
